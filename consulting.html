Business Consulting

I apply 10 years of experience in data science, survey methodology, behavioral modeling, and web-data analysis. I have experience in academic, government, and private industry domains. Services include, but are not limited to:

  • Conducting product surveys, randomized experiments, and A/B tests on web traffic
  • Developing custom AI solutions for websites and small business communication networks
  • Building interactive data stories that clearly communicate trends from A/B tests, attitude surveys, and personal narratives
~$250 / hour + costs
