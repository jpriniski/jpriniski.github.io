Oh, Pinkness!

Towering crystal orbe above pond,

 

the SUBLIME…

the MACABRE…

FOREVER-THING of light…

 

I am for you.

In endless duration, between built space, beyond grasp. Your incalculable presence ringing out as tinnitus. Severed at both sides of now. My naked flesh is yours in pleasure. My movement is yours in dance. Creation is your image.

 

I summon your boundlessness through routine expression, through programmable devotion,  through underexposed graphic iconography. Words hook you to fall flat on their face. No technology can capture your peculiar geometry.

 

 

so unsensible ~ so strange

 

 

Borne as a world with choice. A fearless dance. A circle. The thin air.

The heat in our arms and legs.

 

We touch and play in the field as we all are and have always done and will always do. Rings of naked bodies hand-in-hand fluxing inwards and outwards and inwards like a lung. Daisies and poppies at our ankles. Bees in our hair.

 

Beyond the fragmented media of spacetime, the dragon tree calls out, its fractal fruit a lingering sensation of what is:

 

dead flies and wise dogs

buzz-buzz and click-click

 

is process is lossless

is things you can lick

 

wring out the tonic

that drowns out the old pang

 

before it claws itself out

as a deathening *bang-bang*

 

I jump in the instant, and suck its juices. Bittersweet taste on my tongue. A viscous green liquid trainling down my chest…my stomach…into my pubic hair…onto the ground…

 

I’m left with an impression without memory.

 

a forever-self ~ a no-thing

 

I Inform the voyeurs:

 

“The dance is method is matter is no no-thing at all.”

 

Silence echoes. Songs of bark and green pulp are cast out. Complex understanding has no space for the radiant and pictureless. It’s toward structure :: higher order. Is progress. Is highly complex.

 

The mirror orients. Casting illusions of law-bound appearances. Illuminating the mind’s silent walls with LCD light. It burns a false heat. It plays a lecherous melody in my ear.

It speaks lyrically:

 

Cut short the danse MACABRE

 

“”

 Drown the sweet wring of the SUBLIME

 

So pull your eyes from their sockets and stuff them in your ears. Orientation shields out the incomprehensible the incomparable the indigestible. Media dulls infantile understanding through the hard problem of causality. Believing is time is narrative is society is very scary.

A Babbling baby perceives with coarse measure without memory obscuring the lens. Free from the sticky web of manufactured fluorescence.

Telling what is:

 

“Goo-goo ga-ga.”

 

In time, a mother’s face becomes self-programming becomes becoming becomes mass media becomes violence.

The child plays in the irrigation, drinking the mud. Metallic ecstasy and gritty bare earth. Shapes fall out of the sunlight imaging the imageless. He’s brewed an intoxicating spirit from the aura. Colorless but smelly, it moves without a tempo.

 

It is seen clearly in the irrigation pit.

 

grasp at the milk

be outside the vein

tireless circle in thin air

give no-name

bore the continuum

with many holes

confuse time for matter
for the troll

An ephemeral shadow of maligned shapes dancing about in the aura. Severed from all endpoints. A polished mirror in the sunlight. There it is, as it is. Brilliant and perpetual without reflection.

And as soon as it appears it disappears. Invisible before night settles in.

The road of your own construction is laid straight by analysis by a ruler’s edge. Infrastructure is the imperative of competition. Is coherent is believed.

 

From the process of becoming emerges a network of conquest. An orthogonal grid spraying continuously into the virgin landscape. It gropes her sensitive udder until collapsing.

Senseless experimentation has birthed a diaspora of domination of bored anxiety drowning in ceaseless chatter. Learning is progress is self-programming is confusing. Tear down the gateless gate. Shatter the mirror. Chain the chainless chain. Build a straight path upwards towards the end. Turn dada into intelligible groans of becoming.

I stare at the sun. I’m woozy. I’m drowning.

Embed my social-self in an ever-changing stream of nodes and relations.  Search outwards until I can’t handle anything anymore. Place my head under a bus tire. Pop the nightmare of illusions of grandeur. I’m so light I could float.

 

I go mad sometimes because people are so fucking basic. I deflate because I have nothing new to say.

 

Arrive at your doorstep without traversing the map. Come inside and find a big mess.

Open the window. The air is stale and smells of old cheese.

Kick the door open. Face death creating something new.

 

Step outside and the sun opens our skin from underneath. White noise of a thousand-thousand locust in the trees.

 

Hurl a baseball bat into my back. Penetrate boundaries. Inside is outside is without is within.

 

The fruit calls the bird without knowing its taste. The flower calls the bee without knowing its color. Spanning the spanless span. They are without and without is with all that is.

Poor, poor boy caught in the webbing. Separated and waiting and left thinking. Wet skin covered in goosebumps. Flopping about. So cold. So dry.

 

I pinch myself over and over again and again until exhaustion. A harlequin projects pornography onto my eyeballs. Amorphous structures humping.

A grasshopper with a hotdog as a nose smells my odor. Tells me I should pluck every hair out of my body. I look at the mirror. I paint the mirror lime.

 

Giggling voyeurs point their fingers at me. Voraciously consuming my bare body. Pinching my nipples and tickling me. Up my torso, down my arms.

 

Forgive me, I’m consumed by sexual delusion. Fettered by the outset of desire. I know only time and move only in fear. I’m bred from beyond by a false heat. I’m here but I’m not here. How can I break free with possession rotting my brain?

A distracted search leads back again over again and again. I find myself struck by the sound of it all, jumping crest-to-crest on the waves.
